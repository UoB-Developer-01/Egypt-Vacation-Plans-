Overall Verdict — Highly Recommended

Egypt is one of the world's great travel destinations and exceptional value for Gulf-based travellers. The density of world-class sites within an hour of Giza is unmatched anywhere on Earth. Logistical friction is real but manageable — plan around traffic, book tickets online, travel October–April.

Saqqara Necropolis

🚗 ~30 min south Full day recommended

Oldest stone monument complex on Earth. Step Pyramid of Djoser (2650 BC), Pyramid Texts, Serapeum galleries, and dozens of mastaba tombs with vivid reliefs. 2023 Grand Saqqara upgrades dramatically improved access.

Practical Tips

  • Best season: October–April — summer heat makes outdoor sites brutal before 9 AM or after 4 PM
  • Traffic: Friday morning is best; Thursday evening and weekends on the Ring Road can double travel times
  • Tickets: Book GEM and Egyptian Museum online — weekend queues can be very long
  • Fayoum cluster: Karanis + Lake Qarun + Tunis Village + Wadi El-Rayan in 1–2 nights
  • Southern combo: Saqqara + Dahshur + Memphis covers 5,000 years in one drive
  • Birqash: Go on Friday, arrive before 9 AM — the market winds down by noon
